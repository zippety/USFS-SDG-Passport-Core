## REFLECTIVE EXERCISE 6: VALIDATE BUSINESS MODEL VIABILITY

### REVENUE vs. COST
*   **Year 1 (Pilot):** Revenue $5k (Sponsors) | Cost $1.5k | **CASH FLOW POSITIVE**
*   **Year 2 (Growth):** Revenue $50k (Licensing) | Cost $5k | **HIGH MARGIN**

### KEY SUCCESS FACTORS
1.  **Compliance Need:** Seneca *needs* the data for rankings.
2.  **Viral Loop:** Clubs force members to join, doing our marketing for us.
3.  **Low Cost:** No physical inventory, no logistics.
