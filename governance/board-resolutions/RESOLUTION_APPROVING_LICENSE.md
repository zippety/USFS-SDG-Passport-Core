# RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
## OF SDG INNOVATION LABS (the "Corporation")

**PASSED:** [Date of Meeting]

### APPROVAL OF SOFTWARE LICENSE AGREEMENT

**WHEREAS:**
1.  The Corporation requires software infrastructure to operate its "SDG Passport" and "Living Lab" programs;
2.  **Agape Home Inc.** ("Agape Home") has offered to license its proprietary software to the Corporation for a nominal fee of $1.00 per year;
3.  **Duane Bromfield**, a Director of the Corporation, has disclosed his interest as the sole shareholder of Agape Home and has recused himself from the discussion and vote on this matter in accordance with the *Canada Not-for-profit Corporations Act*;
4.  The remaining Directors have reviewed the proposal and determined that securing this technology at a pro-bono rate is in the best interests of the Corporation.

**BE IT RESOLVED THAT:**

1.  **Approval of Agreement:** The Corporation is authorized to enter into the **Pro-Bono Software License Agreement** with Agape Home Inc. substantially in the form presented to the Board.
2.  **Fairness Determination:** The Board acknowledges that the license fee of $1.00/year is significantly below fair market value and constitutes a benefit to the Corporation.
3.  **Execution:** Any Director or Officer of the Corporation, **other than Duane Bromfield**, is authorized to sign the Agreement on behalf of the Corporation.

---

**CERTIFIED** to be a true copy of a resolution passed by the Board of Directors of the Corporation on the date first written above, at a meeting where a quorum of disinterested directors was present.
