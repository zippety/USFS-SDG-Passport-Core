### 1. The Salary Base ($26,000)
*   **Action:** Pay yourself strictly **$2,166 / month**.

*   **Important:** Avoid dividends if possible.
    *   *Trap:* Dividends are "grossed up" by 15% when calculating benefit eligibility. A $5k dividend looks like $5,750 income to the CRA, killing your Trillium/CWB benefits faster than salary.

### 2. Corporate Asset Usage (Safe) vs. Expensing (Dangerous)

    *   *Result:* CRA charges "Double Tax" + 50% Penalties.

*   **Action:** Set up a **Private Health Services Plan (PHSP)** for the Corporation.
*   **The Benefit:** The Corp pays for your dentist, glasses, and drugs.
    *   Corp gets a **100% Tax Deduction**.
    *   You get a **0% Taxable Benefit**.
*   **Value:** Effectively adds $2k-$5k of tax-free "salary" to your life.
