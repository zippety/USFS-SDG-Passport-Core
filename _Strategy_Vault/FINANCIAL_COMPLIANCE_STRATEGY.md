1.  **The Grant:** SDG Labs (Non-Profit) applies for a grant (e.g., $10k) to run a "Digital Sustainability Workshop."

4.  **The Safeguard:** You (Director) **leave the room**. The *other* board members vote to hire Agape Home because "it is the only platform that offers this specific gamification service."
5.  **The Result:** The grant pays Agape Home. Agape Home pays you a salary.

## üîÆ Option 2: The "Salary Splitting" Model (Future State)
**Concept:** If SDG Labs scales enough to hire staff, you can maximize income by bifurcating your role.

### How it works:
1.  **The Resignation:** You **resign from the Board of Directors**. You cannot be your own boss.
2.  **The Hire:** The Board hires you as the **Executive Director (ED)**.
3.  **The Status:** You are now an *employee*, not a *director*. Employees can be paid a fair salary (e.g., $60k/year).
4.  **The Ownership:** You retain 100% ownership of **Agape Home Inc.** on the side.
5.  **The Result:** You collect a salary from the Non-Profit (as ED) AND profits from the For-Profit (as Owner).
