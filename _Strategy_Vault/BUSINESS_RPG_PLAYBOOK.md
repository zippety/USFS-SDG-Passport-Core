## üåç Chapter 2: Economics ‚Üí "The Server Meta"
*   **The Concept:** The Economy is just the "Game Engine" rules.
*   **The Gig Economy:** **"Mercenary Mode."**
    *   *Meta:* Contractors grinding quests for Gold (Pay) and XP (Resume).
    *   **‚ö†Ô∏è CRA ASSESSMENT WARNING:** Do not misclassify Employees as Contractors (Mercenaries). 
        *   **The Multi-Factor Test:** To stay compliant, the Mods (CRA) check:
            1.  **Control:** Who calls the shots on *how* and *when* work is done?
            2.  **Tools:** Does the Mercenary use their own gear (Laptops/Software)?
            3.  **Profit/Risk:** Can the Mercenary lose money if the quest fails?
            4.  **Subcontracting:** Can they hire their own Alts to do the work?
            5.  **Integration:** Is the worker "part of the guild" or a separate service?
        *   **The Penalty:** If you fail the test, the CRA won't just ban you‚Äîthey will **reassess** for unremitted **source deductions** (CPP/EI and tax), plus **penalties and interest**.
        *   *Source:* [CRA Guide RC4110 - Employee or Self-Employed?](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/publications/rc4110/employee-self-employed.html)
    *   **Ontario Meta:** Adhere to the *Digital Platform Workers' Rights Act*:
        *   **The Buff:** Guarantees minimum wage, right to tips, and **transparency on how pay is calculated.**
        *   **The Nerf:** Applies only to **"Engaged Time"** (Active Questing). Waiting for a quest (Lobby Time) is unpaid. Plan your burn rate accordingly!
        *   *Source:* [Ontario Digital Platform Workers' Rights Act](https://www.ontario.ca/page/digital-platform-workers-rights-act)

## üïπÔ∏è Appendix: The Noob's Glossary
For those who don't speak "Gamer" (Audience Expansion Patch):

*   **Meta (Most Effective Tactic Available):** The current best strategy to win.
*   **Buff / Nerf:** "Buff" is a positive boost (e.g., getting a grant). "Nerf" is a negative change (e.g., higher taxes).
*   **DPS (Damage Per Second):** In business, this is your Revenue speed.
*   **Tank:** A character that takes damage so others remain safe. (The Corporation tanks liability).
*   **Permadeath:** Game Over with no restart. (Bankruptcy is *almost* this, but Hardcore Mode is more accurate).
*   **Aggro:** Attention. "Pulling Aggro" means getting noticed (Marketing).
*   **AoE (Area of Effect):** A spell that hits everyone in a circle, not just one target.
*   **NPC (Non-Player Character):** Employees without guild shares (Equity).
