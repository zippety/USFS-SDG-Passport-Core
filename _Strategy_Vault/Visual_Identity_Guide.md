## ðŸ’Ž Design Elements

### 1. The "Card" Structure (Dashboard Style)
*   **Rule**: Information is organized in clean, white cards with soft shadows (`shadow-md`).
*   **Meaning**: Order and Clarity.
*   **Usage**: Task lists, metrics, event details.

### 2. Typography (The Voice)
*   **Primary Font**: **Inter** (Google Fonts).
*   **Why**: It is the standard for modern, legible user interfaces. It looks clean at small sizes (mobile) and professional at large sizes (presentations).
*   **Weights**: Heavy use of **Bold (700)** for numbers and headers; **Regular (400)** for body text.

### 3. Iconography
*   **Library**: **Lucide React**.
*   **Style**: Simple, outlined, crisp icons. No cartoons.
*   **Usage**: Navigation, Action Buttons, Metric labels.
