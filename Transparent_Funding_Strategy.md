*How to balance profit with purpose.*

*   **Vesting**: Class B shares vest over 4 years. If you leave early, you lose unvested shares. This ensures only *active* contributors benefit from the wealth.

### ğŸ”„ Anti-Corruption Protocol (The "Arm's Length" Rule)
*   **The Problem**: We are Advisors to the Club. If the Club pays **Us** (The Team), it looks like "Corruption."
*   **The Solution (The Bid)**: We must prove **we** are the best option.
*   **The Analogy**: 
    > Imagine the Club needs a plumber. **We** are the plumber.
    > If they just hand **us** cash, it looks suspicious.
    > But if they get a quote from "Joe's Plumbing" ($100) and a quote from **US** ($50), they can legally hire **us**.
    > **The "Bid" is just that piece of paper proving we were the best deal.**

*   **The Process**:
    1.  **Three-Quote Rule**: The Club gets quotes from 2 other developers/vendors. **We** must *win on merit*.
    2.  **Recusal**: The specific team member involved signs a form stating "I abstained from this decision."
    3.  **Specific Deliverables**: Invoices are for concrete outputs (e.g., "App v1.0", "Analytics Dashboard"), not vague "Consulting."
*   **Why This Strengthens Us**: Future investors see we have *internal governance guardrails*. We are disciplined.

## ğŸ¤ Governance: The "Fair Money" Protocol
*How to handle money without destroying relationships.*

**1. Radical Transparency (The Open Ledger)**
*   **The Mechanism**: You don't give them the bank login (security risk). Instead, you hold a **"Monthly Open Book" meeting**.
*   **The Rule**: You show the exact P&L (Profit & Loss). "We earned $10k. Here is the bank statement proving it. We spent $2k on servers. Here is the receipt."
*   **Why**: It proves you aren't hiding profits.


**2. The "30% Safety" Rule**
*   **Rule**: Maintain **3 Months of Operating Runway** in cash at all times. We never drain the account to $0.
*   **Why**: It prevents panic and desperation. We treat "Runway" as a fixed expense.

**3. The "SDG Impact Fund" (The Gift)**
*   **Rule**: The Venture allocates **10% of Net Income (After Tax)** into a restricted "Impact Fund."
*   **Purpose**:
    1.  **Sustain USFS**: Grants to the Seneca Club for events.
    2.  **Global Causes**: Grants to *other* SDG projects.
*   **Why**: You don't need a separate Non-Profit. You just need a specific G/L code and a mission.



**4. Role-Based Pay (Not Status Pay)**
*   **Rule**: We pay for *output*, not titles.
*   **Example**: If a Junior Member codes 50 hours, they get paid. If a Director does 0 hours, they get $0.
*   **Result**: No resentment. Everyone eats what they kill.

**5. The Profit Pool (Dividends & T5s)**
*   **The Rule**: **40% of Net Income** is distributed as **T5 Dividends** to Class B Shareholders based on vested equity.
*   **The Hybrid Pay Model**:
    *   **Salary (T4)**: Base pay for hours worked (Deductible expense for OpEx).
    *   **Dividends (T5)**: Profit share at year-end (Taxed at shareholder level).
*   **Why**: This avoids "Double Taxation" and gives the team the true upside of ownership.
