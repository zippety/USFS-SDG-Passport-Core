## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Work Product"** means any and all inventions, discoveries, developments, improvements, original works of authorship, and other intellectual property, whether or not patentable or registrable under copyright or similar laws, that Contributor creates, conceives, or develops, either alone or jointly with others, during the term of Contributor's relationship with SDG Labs, that:

(a) Relate to the business or actual or demonstrably anticipated research or development of SDG Labs;
(b) Result from any work performed by Contributor for SDG Labs; or
(c) Are developed using SDG Labs' equipment, supplies, facilities, or trade secrets.

**1.2 "SDG Labs IP"** includes but is not limited to:
- SDG Passport platform and all related code
- User interfaces and designs
- Documentation and training materials
- Data structures and databases
- APIs and integrations
- Marketing materials and content

## 2. ASSIGNMENT OF RIGHTS

**2.1 Assignment:** Contributor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to SDG Labs all right, title, and interest in and to all Work Product, including all intellectual property rights therein, throughout the world.

**2.2 Work Made for Hire:** To the extent any Work Product qualifies as "work made for hire" under applicable law, Contributor agrees that SDG Labs shall be considered the author thereof.

**2.3 Future Assignment:** If any Work Product does not qualify as work made for hire, Contributor hereby assigns all rights therein to SDG Labs and agrees to execute any documents necessary to perfect such assignment.

---

## 3. WAIVER OF MORAL RIGHTS

**3.1 Waiver:** To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, Contributor irrevocably waives and agrees never to assert any and all moral rights Contributor may have in any Work Product, including:

(a) The right to be identified as the author;
(b) The right to object to derogatory treatment of the work;
(c) The right to prevent false attribution.

**3.2 Consent:** Contributor consents to SDG Labs using, modifying, adapting, and creating derivative works from any Work Product without attribution.
