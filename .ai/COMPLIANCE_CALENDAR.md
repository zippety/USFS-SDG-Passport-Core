## ðŸ“† MONTHLY TASKS

### 1. CRA Payroll Remittance

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What** | Submit CPP, EI, and income tax deductions to CRA |
| **Who** | Executive Director (ED) |
| **When** | By 15th of the month following payroll |
| **Set Alarm** | 10th of each month |
| **Penalty** | 10% instant penalty for late payment |

**SOP:**
1. Log into CRA My Business Account
2. Navigate to Payroll â†’ Make a Payment
3. Enter total source deductions for previous month
4. Pay via online banking or pre-authorized debit
5. Save confirmation number

**Link:** [CRA My Business Account](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/e-services/e-services-businesses/business-account.html)

---

### 2. Bank Reconciliation

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What** | Match bank transactions to accounting records |
| **Who** | ED (initiates) + Treasurer (approves) |
| **When** | Last business day of each month |
| **Time** | ~30 minutes |

**SOP:**
1. Export bank statement (PDF + CSV)
2. Compare to accounting software/spreadsheet
3. Flag any unmatched transactions
4. Treasurer reviews and approves via email
5. Archive reconciliation report

## ðŸ“† ANNUAL TASKS

### 1. Annual Board Meeting & Budget Approval

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What** | Board meets to approve budget and review ED performance |
| **Who** | Full Board + ED |
| **When** | Q4 (before fiscal year end) |
| **Time** | 1 hour meeting + 30 min prep |
| **Output** | Board Resolution (budget approval) |

**SOP:**
1. ED prepares:
   - Previous year financial summary
   - Proposed budget for next year
   - ED salary recommendation (if applicable)
2. Send to Board 10 days before meeting
3. Hold meeting (in-person, video, or written)
4. Chair calls vote on each item
5. **DJ recuses for any vote on his compensation**
6. Secretary records resolution
7. All Directors sign resolution
8. Archive in `governance/board-resolutions/`

---

### 2. T4 Slips Issued

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What** | Issue T4 slips to all employees |
| **Who** | ED (or payroll service) |
| **When** | By February 28 |
| **Penalty** | $100/day per slip if late |

**SOP:**
1. Generate T4s from payroll system
2. Review for accuracy (total wages, deductions)
3. Submit to CRA electronically
4. Mail or email copies to employees
5. Keep copies for 7 years

**Link:** [CRA T4 Filing](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/tax/businesses/topics/payroll/completing-filing-information-returns/t4-information-employers.html)

---

### 3. T1044 Non-Profit Information Return

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What** | Annual information return for non-profit corporations |
| **Who** | ED |
| **When** | Within 6 months of fiscal year end |
| **Penalty** | $25/day up to $2,500 |

**SOP:**
1. Gather financial statements
2. Complete T1044 form
3. File electronically via My Business Account
4. Save confirmation

**Link:** [T1044 Form](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/forms-publications/forms/t1044.html)

---

### 4. Corporations Canada Annual Return

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **What** | Confirm corporate information is current |
| **Who** | ED (Chair co-signs) |
| **When** | Within 60 days of incorporation anniversary |
| **Fee** | $12 |
| **Penalty** | Corporation can be dissolved for non-filing |

**SOP:**
1. Log into Corporations Canada Online Filing Centre
2. Review and confirm:
   - Registered office address
   - Directors list
   - Corporate name
3. Pay $12 fee
4. Save confirmation

**Link:** [Corporations Canada](https://www.ic.gc.ca/eic/site/cd-dgc.nsf/eng/home)

---
