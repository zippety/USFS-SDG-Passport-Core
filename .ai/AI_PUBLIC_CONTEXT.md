## üõ†Ô∏è PROJECT OVERVIEW
The **SDG Passport** is a web-based decentralized app (dApp) that allows students to:
1.  **Log Impact**: Record actions (planting trees, attending workshops) connected to specific SDGs.
2.  **Earn Verification**: Get validated by campus partners (e.g., responsible cafes, sustainability offices).
3.  **Build a Portfolio**: Generate a "Co-Curricular Transcript" of their impact.
