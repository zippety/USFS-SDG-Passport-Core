## üìã DECISION AUTHORITY MATRIX (RACI)

> **R** = Responsible (does the work)  
> **A** = Accountable (final decision)  
> **C** = Consulted (provides input)  
> **I** = Informed (notified after)

## ‚ö†Ô∏è RECUSAL WORKFLOW

When DJ (as ED) has a conflict of interest:

```
1. DJ identifies conflict (payment to self, contract with Agape Home, etc.)
2. DJ states conflict at board meeting/in resolution
3. DJ leaves the room/call for discussion and vote
4. Remaining directors discuss and vote
5. Decision recorded with "DJ recused" notation
6. DJ returns after vote is complete
```

**Conflicts requiring recusal:**
- ‚ùå DJ's salary or benefits
- ‚ùå Contracts with Agape Home Inc.
- ‚ùå Reimbursements to DJ personally
- ‚ùå Any decision where DJ has financial interest

## üö® EMERGENCY DECISIONS

When urgent action is needed and full board cannot convene:

| Situation | Who Can Act | Requirements |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| **Emergency expense** (<$2k) | ED alone | Document within 48 hours |
| **Emergency expense** (>$2k) | ED + any 1 Director | Email approval, ratify at next meeting |
| **System security breach** | ED alone | Immediate action, notify Board within 24 hours |
| **Legal threat received** | ED | Notify Board Chair immediately |

**Ratification:** All emergency decisions must be ratified at the next board meeting.
