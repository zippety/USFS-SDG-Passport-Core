## ðŸ’Ž Design Elements

### 1. Glassmorphism (The Texture)
*   **Rule**: Our UI elements are not "solid plastic." They are **Frosted Glass**.
*   **Meaning**: Transparency. We have nothing to hide.
*   **Usage**: App Cards, Navigation Bars, Pitch Deck Overlays.

### 2. Organic Curves (The Shape)
*   **Rule**: No sharp 90-degree corners. Everything has a `border-radius`.
*   **Meaning**: Nature doesn't do sharp corners. Neither do we.
*   **Usage**: Buttons are pills. Cards are pebbles.

### 3. Glows & Gradients (The Light)
*   **Rule**: Things shouldn't just "sit" there. They should **emit light**.
*   **Meaning**: You are a being of energy. Your tools should be too.
*   **Usage**: Backgrounds are soft, moving gradients (Aurora Borealis style).
